Our Story

In the heart of the mountains of Minas Gerais, where the aroma of coffee blends with the morning breeze, Café Grão Fino was born. A passion cultivated for generations, turning each bean into a unique experience. From the carefully selected plantation to the artisanal roasting, our mission is to bring you the true ritual of specialty coffee, straight from our farm to your cup.

With respect for the land and traditions, we guarantee a coffee of the highest quality, with rich notes and an unforgettable flavor. We invite you to be part of this journey and discover the story behind every sip.
